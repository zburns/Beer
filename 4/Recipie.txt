
Recipe

Steeping Grain
6oz Caramel 120L
1oz Chocolate
Add both to water immediately and remove at 20 minutes - do not exceed 170 degrees

Malt Extracts
6.6 pounds of Ultralight Malt Extract - boil for 60 minutes - turn off burner before adding

Hops
1 ounce Tettnang Bittering Hops for 55 minutes
1 ounce Willamette Flavoring Hops for last 5 minutes
